Launch Project
Mind Map is a mobile application designed for individuals who take prescription medication for mental health conditions.
The app features a unique and comprehensive mood rating scale, medication reminders, and a daily journal to keep track of your thoughts.
The project goal is to deliver a unique product that supports users' efforts to maintain their mental health.

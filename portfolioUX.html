Case Study #1: Mind Map

Augmenting Healthcare Using Technology


Statistics Canada indicates that 41% of Canadians aged 6-79 use prescription medication.

Prescription medication use is associated with physical and mental health difficulties.

The majority of prescription medication users report poor emotional well-being.

1 in 4 Canadians are on prescription medication and feel generally unhappy.





How can we improve the well-being of this demographic?


What are they saying?

What are they doing?

What are they feeling?

What are they thinking?

What are their pain points?

What do they stand to gain?

How can we turn their pains into gains?

Designing the Solution

Introducing...

Keep track of your prescriptions.

Set pill reminders.

View your weekly and monthly mood ratings.

Remain mindful of your energy levels.

Express yourself in writing.

Get to the core of your emotions.

Gain self-awareness and self-acceptance.

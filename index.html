about me
Hi, hi. My name is Anna and I want to use technology to improve mental health.

I graduated from the University of Waterloo in 2017 with an Honours BA in Psychology. During my time in school, I managed a research lab devoted to the study of personality and intimate relationships.
My post academic life hasn't unfolded as gracefully as I'd imagined it would in my rose coloured mind. After forgoing a long-held dream to practice clinical therapy, my identity somehow lost its sense of vibrancy.

Where would I place my ambition?

My empathy?

How will I go on?
Romantics are dramatic but they always turn the narrative around.

I've since fallen in love with computer programming and user experience design. In 2019, I began developing my first mobile application, Mind Map.

To learn more about my story, take a look at my resume .

Launch Project
Mind Map is a mobile application designed for individuals who take prescription medication for mental health conditions.
The app features a unique and comprehensive mood rating scale, medication reminders, and a daily journal to keep track of your thoughts.
The project goal is to
deliver a unique product that supports users'
efforts to maintain their mental health.
